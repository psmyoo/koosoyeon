About

Koo Soyeon is a Senior Environmental Analyst with over eight years of experience in air quality monitoring and pollutant analysis.

Specializing in industrial emissions and ambient air assessment, she applies rigorous analytical methodologies to deliver data that withstands regulatory scrutiny. Her work spans the full measurement lifecycle — from instrument calibration and sample collection through laboratory analysis, quality assurance, and compliance reporting.
